About Us

The Classic Car Centre has been exclusively restoring and maintaining classic cars in Ireland for last thirty five years. We are probably the longest established classic car restoration and repair garage in the country.

We started out in a small rented workshop in Rathmines and then spent some years in Dun Laoghaire. In 1998 we re-located to Kilcullen, in Co. Kildare before moving to Wexford in 2007.
